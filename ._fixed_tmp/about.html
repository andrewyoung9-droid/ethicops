About EthicOps

We build ethical, privacy-respecting tools because performance and dignity shouldn’t be trade-offs. From Minnesota to the wider world, our mission is to help people feel safer, in control, and respected online.

Our Mission

EthicOps was founded with a simple idea: technology should protect people, not exploit them. We stand with the community in Minnesota and beyond to defend digital rights, empower everyday users, and make privacy accessible for all.

Our Kame-Ha Optimizer and other tools are designed to give you performance without surveillance, security without compromise, and trust without question.

We believe in building a future where ethical software empowers individuals, and we’re proud to call Minnesota our home base while serving people everywhere.

Community first

EthicOps grew out of neighbors helping neighbors. We support everyday people—students, parents, and small businesses—who deserve computers that work hard for them without spying on them.

Privacy with dignity

We believe privacy is a civil right. That’s why Kame-Ha ships without telemetry, and why our website collects only what’s necessary. Your trust is our most valuable asset.

Minnesota roots, open arms

We’re proud of our Minnesota roots: practical, steady, community-minded. We share guides and tools so anyone can take back performance and peace of mind—here at home and anywhere people need it.
