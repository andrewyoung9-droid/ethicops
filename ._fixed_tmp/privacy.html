Privacy Policy

Effective 2025-08-18 — plain-English summary of what we do and don’t collect.

1. What we collect

  • Software: No telemetry, analytics, or background beacons.
  • Website: No analytics cookies. Web server logs (IP, user agent, time) are retained briefly for security and abuse prevention.
  • Payments: Processed by PayPal. We receive your transaction record (amount, time, status) but not your card number.
  • Email: If you email us, we will keep your message to respond and for support history.

2. Use of information

  • Provide downloads, process orders, offer support, fight fraud/abuse.
  • Publish anonymous stats (e.g., number of downloads) — never personal data.

3. Data retention

  • Server logs: short-term rotation.
  • Support tickets/emails: retained as needed for ongoing support.
  • Payment records: kept for accounting and legal compliance.

4. Your choices

  • Don’t want email retained? Ask us to delete support threads once resolved.
  • Verification options include Authenticode, SHA-256, and PGP signatures (see Trust).

5. Security

All official downloads are signed. The website enforces a strict Content Security Policy and avoids third-party scripts where possible.

6. Contact

Email privacy@ethicops.gt.tc. PGP key & fingerprints are on the Trust page.
