Verify the signature (PGP)

Import our public key and verify the .asc signature.

# 1) Save our public key to EthicOps-publickey.asc
# 2) Import
gpg --import EthicOps-publickey.asc
# 3) Verify
gpg --verify KameHaSetup.exe.asc KameHaSetup.exe
#    or for the portable zip:
gpg --verify KameHa_Portable.zip.asc KameHa_Portable.zip
